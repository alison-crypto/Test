SOPs — quick reference

🌙
Sunday night setup
15min · sets up the whole week
  1. Garbage + recycling out to building bins
  2. Pack Alison's gym bag for Tuesday
  3. Pre-portion breakfast for Tue AM
  4. Fill coffee machine + set timer for 5:25am Tue
  5. Lay out gym clothes for both
  6. Phones to charger across the room
  7. Alarm set: 5:30am Tue
🏋
Gym morning sequence
5:30 → 7:30 weekdays · ~8:30 → noon weekends
  1. 5:30 — alarm · coffee + whey shake (3min)
  2. 5:35 — change into pre-laid gym clothes
  3. 5:45 — out the door, drive to YMCA Westhills (~8min)
  4. 6:00 — scan in, both walk in
  5. 6:00–6:50 — workout (50min, follow plan in tracker)
  6. 6:50 — drive home (~8min)
  7. 7:00 — Alison: shower, dress, grab pre-packed breakfast · Darlene: change, slow morning
  8. 7:20 — Alison out the door · 7:30 at desk, breakfast there
  9. Weekends: same sequence, starting 8:30am wake, 9:00 gym open, no work after
🍳
Sunday meal prep
2-phase split around pool · see Household_Plan_v14.docx for full recipes
  1. Phase 1 (8:00–9:15) — before pool:
  2. 8:00 — coffee, light breakfast
  3. 8:15 — load slow-cooker meal (longest cook starts now)
  4. 8:45 — mix marinades, get rice/grains going if needed
  5. 9:15 — lock up, walk out to YMCA
  6. 🏊 Pool 10–11am · 🚶 Walk home ~11:30
  7. Phase 2 (11:45–1:30) — after pool:
  8. 11:45 — quick snack (apple/yogurt — slow-cookers should be nearly done)
  9. 12:00 — sheet pans into oven, chop fresh veg
  10. 12:45 — portion into containers, label with date
  11. 1:15 — clean kitchen, restock fridge
  12. 1:30 — done, lunch together

  1. Scoop litter into small bag kept beside box

Pregnancy ramp — chores shift as Darlene tires

Schedule above assumes Darlene at wk 26+1, energy still good. As pregnancy progresses, Alison takes more on. Alison's mom arrives postpartum for 1–2 months to help.

  • Now (wk 26–30): Schedule as written.
  • Wk 30–34: Alison adds bathroom cleans + vacuum edges (bending-heavy tasks).
  • Wk 34–40: Alison takes ALL weekly chores. Darlene does daily only.
  • Postpartum wk 0–8: Mom + Alison handle everything. Darlene heals + feeds baby.
  • Recovery wk 8–12+: Renegotiate based on Darlene's capacity, baby's sleep, what's sustainable.

Time accounting

  • Darlene chore time/week: ~30–40min daily × 7 = ~3.5–4.5h + weekly tasks ~2.5h = ~6–7h total
  • Alison chore time/week: Cat care 35min + Sunday prep 4h + groceries 1h + garbage 10min = ~5.5h total
  • Together on house stuff: Dinner cleanup × 7 + Sat groceries + Sat date dinner = ~3h

The split looks "her does more" because she's home full-time. Her ~7h chores vs. Alison's ~45h/week at the office is the real balance. If she ever feels overwhelmed, the pregnancy ramp above kicks in earlier.
