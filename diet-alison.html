How it works

🔥 Ghost: full scoop on hard days (Tue/Fri/Sat), ½ scoop other days.

🥩 Protein swap: 150g chicken = 160g turkey = 180g lean beef. On HIGH/PEAK, if beef, drop 1 tbsp oil.

🥤 MRE = 1 Redcon1 serving (4 scoops = 46P / 60C / 6F). Also fine to swap into any breakfast/snack when rushed.

🍚 All meat/rice/potato = cooked weight. One starch per plate. Still hungry → add 20g oats or a banana.
