🏃 Hyrox Prep

Tuesday · ~50 min · compromised-run circuit · Vancouver Dec
Warm-up8 min
Main set~36 min
Cool-down6 min

Warm-up · 8 min

  • 3 min easy treadmill jog or row
  • Dynamic flow (1 round): 10 leg swings/side · 10 walking lunges w/ twist · 5 inchworm→push-up · 10 air squats · 10 scap pull-ups / band pull-aparts
  • Primer (2 min): 2 rounds of 5 broad jumps + 5 burpees — wakes the pattern up before you're tired
  • 🦶 Add ankle circles + a set of calf raises · 🔒 a few braced good-mornings to prime the lower back

Main set · Compromised-Run Circuit

Run 400m, then straight into the station — keep transitions under 30 sec (that's the skill). Start at 6 rounds; build toward 8 as December nears. Pace the runs "comfortably hard" — never sprint the first ones.

Cool-down · 6 min

2-min easy walk or row, then hold ~45 sec each:

  • Couch stretch (quads/hip flexors — you'll need these after the lunges)
  • Pigeon (glutes/hips) · Lat / overhead reach · Calves (your ankle)

Finish with 1 min slow nasal breathing to drop the heart rate.

Your cues

🔒 Lower back: broad jumps, KB swings and lunges load it — brace hard. If it niggles, swap broad jumps for step-back lunges and drop swing load.

🦶 Left ankle: wrap it for heavy carries/lunges; control every landing on the broad jumps.

🏃 Pace & fuel: runs "comfortably hard" — races are lost on the sleds & wall balls. This is a HIGH-carb day; Monday's dinner + breakfast fuel it.

🔁 Rotate the cut: when doing 6 rounds, rotate which 2 stations you skip so you still practise lunges & wall balls regularly. Don't add a 2nd Hyrox session/week yet — one is right for your load.
