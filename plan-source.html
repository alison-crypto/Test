Breakfast

  • 2 medium boiled eggs (100 g)
  • 3 tbsp lactose-free low-fat cottage cheese (75 g)
  • 2 slices whole wheat bread (50 g)
  • 1 medium pear (110 g)
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds (30 g)

Substitutions

  • For cottage cheese: 1 tbsp lactose-free cream cheese (15 g) OR 3 tbsp lactose-free light ricotta cream (30 g)
  • For whole wheat bread: 3 whole grain toasts (30 g)
  • For pear: 1 medium banana (40 g) OR 10 medium strawberries (120 g)

Notes: Mash the boiled eggs and mix them with the cottage cheese to create a spread for the bread. Season with herbs of your choice.

Lunch

  • 150 g grilled chicken breast
  • 5 heaping tbsp cooked brown rice (100 g)
  • 2 heaping tbsp cooked lentils (54 g)
  • 2 serving spoons steamed vegetables (110 g)
  • Mixed salad (lettuce, tomato, and onion) (70 g)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil (8 mL)
  • 1 small orange (90 g)

Substitutions

  • For grilled chicken breast: 3 tbsp sautéed lean ground beef (75 g) OR 6 tbsp shredded chicken (120 g)
  • For brown rice: 1½ medium ladles cooked whole wheat pasta (112 g) OR 2 serving spoons mashed potatoes (160 g) OR 2½ serving spoons pumpkin purée (200 g) OR 2½ small slices cooked sweet potato (100 g) OR 2 small pieces cooked cassava (100 g)
  • For lentils: 4 tbsp cooked pinto beans (68 g) OR 4 tbsp cooked black beans (68 g) OR 3 level tbsp cooked chickpeas (45 g)

Afternoon Snack

  • 1 medium banana (40 g)
  • 3 tbsp oat bran (30 g)
  • 1 scoop whey isolate protein (30 g)

Preparation: Blend all ingredients with water to make a smoothie.

Substitutions

  • For banana: 0.7 small apple (58 g) OR 7 large strawberries (145 g)

Dinner

  • 2½ heaping tbsp sautéed lean ground beef (62.5 g)
  • 1 whole wheat wrap (30 g)
  • 2 serving spoons steamed vegetables (110 g)
  • 2 leaves iceberg lettuce (48 g)
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil (8 mL)
  • 1 medium slice pineapple (75 g)

Substitutions

  • For lean ground beef: 1 small grilled or baked fish fillet (100 g) OR 6 tbsp shredded chicken (120 g) OR 2 medium boiled eggs (100 g)
  • For whole wheat wrap: 1½ serving spoons mashed potatoes (120 g) OR 4 heaping tbsp cooked brown rice (80 g)

Evening Snack

  • 1 serving plain full-fat Greek yogurt (100 g)
  • 2 dessert spoons ground flaxseed (20 g)

Important Reminders

  • Eat at least three main meals per day (breakfast, lunch, dinner) and two to three healthy snacks between meals.
  • Have small snacks throughout the day based on whole and minimally processed foods.
  • Include fruits, vegetables, and leafy greens every day.
  • Eat beans or other legumes daily, especially combined with rice.
  • If you don't eat meat, combine legumes with vitamin C-rich foods or yellow-orange fruits and veggies to boost iron absorption.
  • Limit salt and processed seasonings.
  • Drink water regularly — 2.5 – 3.0 L daily.

Foods and Beverages to Avoid

  • Sugary beverages (soda, powdered drinks, boxed juices). Limit artificial sweeteners.
  • Ultra-processed foods (instant noodles, packaged snacks, filled cookies, candies).
  • Fast food.
  • Fried foods and high-fat preparations.
  • Excess sugar and sweets.
  • Alcoholic beverages.
